About Us

Who are we?

Flyingwheelchair.com is a personalized flight search and booking platform dedicated to travelers with mobility devices that focuses on their individual accessibility needs.

We are travel accessibility and technology experts on a mission to empower everyone to fly without limits.

What is our goal?

Our goal is to deliver an unparalleled, user-centric booking process as well as a friction-less travel experience.

Our dream is to allow millions of wheelchair users to book flights with confidence.

How we do it?

  • We capture and understand travelers' accessibility needs. In just a few clicks you will create your detailed profile. Promised, you won't need to do it again, ever.
  • We leverage the new NDC standard to connect directly with airlines, gathering the right information and communicating your needs. Be assured that they know about YOU.
  • We aggregate a unique set of external data sources. Because you have the right to make an informed decision.
